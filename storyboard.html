The Theme

This website has a modern minimalistic theme, the colors used suggest a balance of professionalism and calmness (from the primary and secondary colors) with a touch of warmth and approachability (from the tertiary color). The target audience of this website people who are seeking new hires and those who would like to know more about Justin.
