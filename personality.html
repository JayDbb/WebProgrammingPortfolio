How did Justin become Justin?

Academic Life

I was not always a good student. To this day I can't say what happened that caused the change, but in the 9th grade (during covid) is when my grades started to pick up pace, my hero is also to be thanked as he supported me. Around this time is when I really started to find a interest in programming.

My Addictive Personality

My addictive personality trait caused me to focus on programming allowing me to learn multifarious programming concepts. Along with that, my addiction to programming lead me to watch day in the life of people who program for a living. Seeing the people live my dream fueled my hunger to do well so I could live that life. So I believe that my addictive personality and hobby inadvertently impacted my school life.

My Hero

My hero would have to be my father. Reason being he is a quintessential example of what a man should be as a father, a husband, a worker and a generally good person. I chose him because I want to be like him when I grow up. One thing he always says that sticks with me is “Nobody is coming”. What I understand it to mean is that this world is very selfish and you have to be able to support yourself as little to nobody is coming to you're rescue, friends or family, so you must work smart and make reasonable life choices.
